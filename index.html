About Me

I'm a PhD Candidate at University of California, Irvine, in the department of Earth System Science. My reserach is focused the nexus of vegetation cover change, fire risk, and carbon in Californian ecosystems.

Research

Projects

  • Are Californian forests recovering slower after fire?
  • How are changing fuels in the state influencing fire risk? How does fire risk return after fire?
  • How have aboveground live carbon stocks changed in the past 40 years?
